Frequently asked questions

  • What will you do to prevent false accident reports 
    The helmet sends an alert to the website 10 seconds later. We can create a system when the helmet detects an accident the helmet vibrates, starts beeping and notificationis sent to the riders mobile. The rider understands that it is a false accident detection and can cancel the the accident report through his mobile or a gesture on the helmet. 
  • Will the price of helmet be affordable to all the people of the society?
    If this product becomes successful and we get enough support we can mass produce the helmet and automatically the price of the helmet will brought down and will affordable to the whole society
  • Will the helmet be battery operated or charged?
    The helmet will have a battery pack which which will be charged through a battery pack. A battery life of 10-12 hours can be expected.
  • With all these sensors how will the weight of the helmet be managed?
    All the sensors are small and the weight of the helmet will be reduced as much as possible so that it is comfortable and not heavy on the head 
  • Will the alert sent from website be done manually or is it all AI based?
     The alert sent is all AI based and no manual input is required.

About

Safety helmet is a type of protective headgear used by the rider which makes bike driving safer than before. The main purpose of this helmet is to provide safety for the rider. This can be implemented by using advanced features like accident indentification, location tracking, use as a hands free device, fall detection.
