Testimonials

One of the major causes of death in the present world is not any disease but road accidents. Drunken driving is one of the major causes of death amongst youngsters. The smart helmet is an idea proposed by this team in the field of technology in the advanced automobile industry. It is a novel concept in the right direction to reduce fatality during road accidents. Proper implementation of this idea would defiantly save the lives of many people involved in road accidents. They say prevention is better than cure. In the case of an accident, the smart helmet will be able to notify a nearby medical facility for assistance. In the meantime, it will make sure that any person will not be able to drink and drive when the helmet is on. In this way, it will bring down the cases of accidents.

About

Safety helmet is a type of protective headgear used by the rider which makes bike driving safer than before. The main purpose of this helmet is to provide safety for the rider. This can be implemented by using advanced features like accident indentification, location tracking, use as a hands free device, fall detection.
